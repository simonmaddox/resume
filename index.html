Background

I am a mobile developer with lots of experience in building applications for most platforms. I have worked with some of the biggest brands and agencies in the world, producing hit mobile products and helping to define mobile strategy.

I have built and released one of the most popular iOS apps in the UK - 0870 - which, to date, has saved consumers over £15 million. It has been featured in a wide variety of the UK press, including The Guardian, The Sunday Times, The Telegraph, T3 Magazine, and many many more.

I have been featured in Wired magazine as an iPhone developer "expert", and have worked with Microsoft in briefing journalists on development during the Windows Phone 7 launch.
